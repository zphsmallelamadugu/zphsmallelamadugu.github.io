— About Our School

A government school that keeps village dreams alive.

For more than thirty years, ZPHS Mallelamadugu has been the quiet backbone of education in our mandal. We teach classes 6 through 10 — preparing students from surrounding villages for board examinations, competitive scholarships and life beyond.

Discipline, regular attendance and respect for elders are woven into daily school life. Children who pass through our corridors are known across the region for their composure and clarity of thought.

We are a fully government-aided Zilla Parishad High School — which means every child studies without tuition fees, receives free textbooks and uniforms, and a warm mid-day meal each school day.
